### Prime Number Validation:
It is the simple act of verifying if a number is prime or not.<br/>
What is a prime number?<br/>
A number that can be divided by itself and by 1.<br/>
#### Prime Number and Cryptography<br/>
For the use of the RSA algorithm: we need to verify if the numbers used are prime numbers.<br/><br/>
<strong>RSA</strong><strong> only works with prime numbers</strong>.<br/>
<strong>Bitcoins</strong> also use prime numbers.<br/>
The <strong>SSH passwords generation</strong> also need to use prime Numbers.<br/><br/>
